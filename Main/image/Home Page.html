About Us

Newly formed Uttarakhand is the 27th state ofIndia having 13 districts with population of 84.89 Lakhs according to the 2001 census. In the light of adverse geographical circumstances of hilly areas and increase in the availability of technical education, fast establishment of Government Polytechnics began after formation of the State. Till date the State has AICTE approved 36 Government Polytechnics, in the Kumaon and Garhwal regions.

This Polytechnic was established by Government of Uttarakhand in December 30, 2005 for three Diploma courses namely Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Civil Engineering. Presently we are running a Computer Science and Engineering only. The classes of Information Technology of Government Girls’ Polytechnic Jakholi are also running in this Institute.

The Institute is situated on Rishikesh-Badrinath National Highway-58, at village Ratura, District Rudraprayag. We have 2.881 hectare land and the process of construction of building is in progress.

Keeping in view the vast progress in the field of industrialization in this state, Polytechnics have to redefine themselves and come with shoulder to shoulder status with the industries. We are trying to develop human resource with same pace as industrial growth is going on. Quality in our students is because of our policies to develop skill and will among them due to:

* We enhance the competence of the faculty and support staff through SWOT analysis and STC.

* We produce manpower for industry/market needs.

* We support to Board for development and implementation of Learning Materials/Syllabus as per the need of industry.

* We improve quality of teaching on continuous basis through feedback from industry and students.

* We motivate students through rewards, periodic personality development & entrepreneurship programmes.

* We use ICT in teaching and learning like Gyan Darshan, Mobile learning, PPTs and videos.

* We continuous strengthen our labs, library with the help of funds received from State as well as MHRD, GOI under Sub-Mission Scheme.

* We continuous upgrade and maintain laboratory facilities.

* We invite Industries are giving placements through campus interviews.

* We are generating the feeling of more belongingness for the institute and enhance the ethical status.

* We have created ragging free and smoke free environment.
